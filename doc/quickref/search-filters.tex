{\Large\bfseries\color{maki-salmon} MAKI Search Filter Reference}
\end{center}
\vspace{2pt}

All filters work in \cmd{maki search}, the web UI search box, and saved searches.
Filters combine with \textbf{AND}. Free-text tokens search names, filenames, descriptions, and metadata.

\vspace{6pt}

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.1}
\small
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{}l l X@{}}
\textbf{Filter} & \textbf{Syntax} & \textbf{Example} \\[2pt]
\hline\\[-6pt]
Asset type          & \cmd{type:<type>}                     & \cmd{type:image}, \cmd{type:video} \\
Tag                 & \cmd{tag:<name>}                      & \cmd{tag:landscape}, \cmd{tag:"Fools Theater"} \\
Tag whole path      & \cmd{tag:=<name>}                     & \cmd{tag:=Legoland} (exact value, not leaves elsewhere) \\
Tag leaf only       & \cmd{tag:/<name>}                     & \cmd{tag:/animal|bird} (no descendants) \\
Tag case-sensitive  & \cmd{tag:\textasciicircum<name>}      & \cmd{tag:\textasciicircum Landscape} (not "landscape") \\
Tag prefix anchor   & \cmd{tag:|<text>}                     & \cmd{tag:|wed} (wedding, wedding-2024, ...) \\
Exclude tag         & \cmd{-tag:<name>}                     & \cmd{-tag:rejected} \\
Format              & \cmd{format:<ext>}                    & \cmd{format:nef}, \cmd{format:jpg} \\
Rating              & \cmd{rating:<N>}, \cmd{<N>+}, \cmd{<N>-<M>}, \cmd{<N>,<M>} & \cmd{rating:4+}, \cmd{rating:3-5}, \cmd{rating:0} (unrated) \\
Color label         & \cmd{label:<color>}, \cmd{label:none}  & \cmd{label:Red}, \cmd{label:none} \\
Camera              & \cmd{camera:<text>}                   & \cmd{camera:fuji}, \cmd{camera:"Canon EOS R5"} \\
Lens                & \cmd{lens:<text>}                     & \cmd{lens:56mm}, \cmd{lens:"RF 50mm"} \\
Description         & \cmd{description:<text>}, \cmd{desc:<text>} & \cmd{description:sunset} \\
ISO                 & \cmd{iso:<N>}, \cmd{iso:<min>-<max>}  & \cmd{iso:100}, \cmd{iso:100-800} \\
Focal length        & \cmd{focal:<N>}, \cmd{focal:<min>-<max>} & \cmd{focal:50}, \cmd{focal:35-70} \\
Aperture            & \cmd{f:<N>}, \cmd{f:<min>-<max>}      & \cmd{f:2.8}, \cmd{f:1.4-2.8} \\
Width / Height      & \cmd{width:<N>+}, \cmd{height:<N>+}   & \cmd{width:4000+}, \cmd{height:2000+} \\
Source metadata     & \cmd{meta:<key>=<value>}              & \cmd{meta:software=CaptureOne} \\
Path pattern        & \cmd{path:<pattern>} (\cmd{*}=wild)   & \cmd{path:*/2026/*/party} \\
Collection          & \cmd{collection:<name>}               & \cmd{collection:Favorites} \\
Date                & \cmd{date:<prefix>}                   & \cmd{date:2026}, \cmd{date:2026-02} \\
Date from / until   & \cmd{dateFrom:<date>}, \cmd{dateUntil:<date>} & \cmd{dateFrom:2026-01-01} \\
Volume              & \cmd{volume:<label>}                  & \cmd{volume:Archive}, \cmd{volume:none} \\
Asset ID            & \cmd{id:<prefix>}                     & \cmd{id:72a0bb4b} \\
Copies              & \cmd{copies:<N>}, \cmd{copies:<N>+}   & \cmd{copies:1}, \cmd{copies:2+} \\
Variants            & \cmd{variants:<N>+}                   & \cmd{variants:2+} \\
Tag count (leaves)  & \cmd{tagcount:<N>}, \cmd{tagcount:<N>+}, \cmd{tagcount:<A>-<B>} & \cmd{tagcount:0}, \cmd{tagcount:5+} \\
Scattered           & \cmd{scattered:<N>+}, \cmd{scattered:<N>+/<depth>} & \cmd{scattered:2+/1} \\
Duration (seconds)  & \cmd{duration:<N>}, \cmd{duration:<N>+}, \cmd{duration:<min>-<max>} & \cmd{duration:60}, \cmd{duration:30+} \\
Codec               & \cmd{codec:<text>}                    & \cmd{codec:h264}, \cmd{codec:hevc} \\
GPS location        & \cmd{geo:<S>,<W>,<N>,<E>}             & \cmd{geo:47.5,11.0,48.5,13.0} \\
GPS any/none        & \cmd{geo:any}, \cmd{geo:none}         & \cmd{geo:any} \\
Orphan              & \cmd{orphan:true}                     & \cmd{orphan:true} \\
Missing files       & \cmd{missing:true}                    & \cmd{missing:true} \\
Stale verification  & \cmd{stale:<days>}                    & \cmd{stale:30} \\
Stacked             & \cmd{stacked:true}, \cmd{stacked:false} & \cmd{stacked:true} \\
Face count \pro     & \cmd{faces:any}, \cmd{faces:none}, \cmd{faces:<N>+} & \cmd{faces:2+} \\
Person \pro         & \cmd{person:<name>}                   & \cmd{person:Alice} (repeat for AND, comma for OR) \\[6pt]
Visual similarity \pro  & \cmd{similar:<id>}, \cmd{similar:<id>:<limit>} & \cmd{similar:72a0bb4b:50} \\
Similarity threshold \pro & \cmd{min\_sim:<percent>}           & \cmd{min\_sim:90} \\
Text search \pro    & \cmd{text:<query>}                    & \cmd{text:"sunset beach"} \\
Embedding status \pro & \cmd{embed:any}, \cmd{embed:none}   & \cmd{embed:none type:image} \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{6pt}
All numeric filters support: exact (\cmd{3}), minimum (\cmd{3+}), range (\cmd{3-5}), OR (\cmd{2,4}).

\section{Combining Filters}

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.4}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{}l X@{}}
\textbf{AND} & Repeat a filter to require all values \\
             & \cmd{tag:landscape tag:sunset} \\[6pt]
\textbf{OR}  & Comma within a single filter \\
             & \cmd{tag:alice,bob} \quad \cmd{format:nef,cr3} \\[6pt]
\textbf{NOT} & Dash prefix excludes matches \\
             & \cmd{-tag:rejected} \quad \cmd{-format:xmp} \\[6pt]
\textbf{Combined} & \cmd{type:image,video -tag:rejected rating:3+} \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{4pt}
Hierarchical tags: \cmd{tag:bird} matches at any level --- \cmd{animal|bird}, \cmd{animal|bird|eagle}, and standalone \cmd{bird}.

\vspace{18pt}
\section{Sort Options}

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.35}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{}l X@{}}
\cmd{date\_desc}  & Newest first \textit{(default)} \\
\cmd{date\_asc}   & Oldest first \\
\cmd{name\_asc}   & Name A\textrightarrow Z \\
\cmd{name\_desc}  & Name Z\textrightarrow A \\
\cmd{size\_desc}  & Largest first \\
\cmd{size\_asc}   & Smallest first \\
\cmd{similarity\_desc} & Most similar first \pro \\
\cmd{similarity\_asc}  & Least similar first \pro \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{4pt}
Usage: \cmd{maki search "rating:4+" --sort name\_asc}

\vspace{18pt}
\section{Output Formats}

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.35}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{}l X@{}}
\textit{(default)}       & ID, filename, type, format, date \\
\cmd{--format ids} / \cmd{-q} & One UUID per line (scripting) \\
\cmd{--format full}      & Default + tags, description \\
\cmd{--format json}      & JSON array \\
\cmd{--format '\{..\}'}  & Custom template \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{4pt}
{\small Placeholders: \cmd{\{id\}}, \cmd{\{name\}}, \cmd{\{filename\}}, \cmd{\{type\}}, \cmd{\{format\}}, \cmd{\{tags\}}, \cmd{\{description\}}, \cmd{\{rating\}}, \cmd{\{label\}}, \cmd{\{date\}}, \cmd{\{size\}}}

\section{Path Normalization}

The \cmd{path:} filter auto-normalizes in the CLI:

\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.35}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{}l X@{}}
\cmd{\textasciitilde}  & Expands to home directory \\
\cmd{./} \cmd{../}     & Resolves relative to cwd \\
\cmd{/Volumes/...}     & Strips volume mount, applies \cmd{volume:} \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{18pt}
\section{Common Recipes}

\recipe{rating:4+ type:image}{Best images}
\recipe{date:2026-03 tag:landscape}{March landscapes}
\recipe{format:nef -tag:processed}{Unprocessed RAWs}
\recipe{copies:1 volume:Photos}{Single-copy files (backup candidates)}
\recipe{stale:90}{Not verified in 90 days}
\recipe{orphan:true}{Assets with no file locations}
\recipe{faces:any person:Alice rating:4+}{Alice's best shots}
\recipe{similar:72a0bb4b rating:3+ min\_sim:80 \pro{}}{Visually similar, curated}
\recipe{text:"sunset on the beach" min\_sim:70 \pro{}}{Semantic image search}
